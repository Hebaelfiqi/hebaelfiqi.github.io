Research group and supervision

Honours and undergraduate research

Between 2018 and 2024 I also supervised more than a dozen honours and fourth-year engineering research projects at UNSW Canberra, on topics including shepherding-based patrolling, reinforcement learning for navigation and air-to-air scenarios, swarm countermeasures, and object detection on resource-limited robots. Several of these projects led to peer-reviewed publications with the students as first authors.
